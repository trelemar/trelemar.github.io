BlueSky

The Narrowing is a head-to-head card game about hidden information and tracking. Led by your compass, players plot raids on their opponent’s hidden Paths, attempting to locate and capture their Captains. Creatures help you gather Secrets to fuel your raids, but committing too much can leave you exposed. Each raid narrows the possibilities, tightening where your opponent can hide. Find their Captains before the Paths close in on you.
