The Narrowing is a head-to-head card game of combat, deduction, and bluffing. Led by a compass, players Plot Raids on their opponent’s hidden Paths to locate and capture their Captains. Creatures help gather Secrets to fuel Raids, but committing too much can leave them Exposed. Each Raid narrows the possibilities, tightening where Captains can hide. Find their Captains before your own Paths close in on you.
